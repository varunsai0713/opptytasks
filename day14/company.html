List of Top Cryptocurrency Companies Worldwide:

Advanced Micro Devices Inc.

Establishment: 1969

Headquarters: United States

Advanced Micro Devices Inc. is a multinational semiconductor company that develops computer processors and related technologies for business and consumer markets. The company also develops high-performance computing and visualization products such as microprocessors, motherboard chipsets, embedded processors, and graphics processors for servers, PC, workstation, and embedded system applications. Moreover, it offers the best combination of CRU and GPU technologies to make more secure and faster blockchain transactions.

Alphapoint Corporation

Establishment: 2013

Headquarters: United States

AlphaPoint Corporation is a white-label software company powering crypto exchanges worldwide. The company is the only technology provider in the crypto exchange platform space that offers software solutions for both primary issuance and secondary trading. It also provides a secure, scalable, and customizable digital asset trading platform which has enabled over 150 customers in 35 countries to launch and operate crypto markets and digitize assets.

Bitfury Group Limited

Establishment: 2011

Headquarters: Netherlands

Bitfury Group Limited is a full-service blockchain technology company and one of the largest private infrastructure providers in the blockchain ecosystem. The company develops and delivers both hardware and software solutions for businesses, governments, organizations, and individuals to securely move assets across the blockchain. It also provides a wide range of services, including consulting, engineering, and research and development.

Blockdaemon Inc.

Establishment: 2017

Headquarters: United States

Blockdaemon Inc. is a leading independent blockchain infrastructure platform that enables enterprises to easily deploy and manage nodes and networks. The company provides a secure and reliable platform for businesses to build, scale, and operate blockchain applications. It supports a wide range of blockchain protocols, including Bitcoin, Ethereum, Polkadot, and more.

Coinbase Inc.

Establishment: 2012

Headquarters: United States

The company aims to build a crypto economy as a more fair, accessible, efficient, and transparent financial system that is enabled by crypto. It was started with the radical idea that anyone, anywhere, should be able to send and receive Bitcoin easily and securely. It offers a trusted and easy-to-use platform for accessing the broader crypto economy. The platform allows merchants, consumers, and traders to facilitate transactions with digital currency.
