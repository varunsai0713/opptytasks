Major companies in the cryptocurrency and blockchain industry include Binance, Coinbase, Ripple, Block, and IBM, which operate across various sectors such as exchanges, payment systems, and enterprise technology.

These companies can be categorized by their primary function:

Cryptocurrency Exchanges and Financial Services

These firms offer platforms for buying, selling, and managing cryptocurrencies, along with related financial services.

  • Binance: This is the largest cryptocurrency exchange globally by trading volume. It also has its own blockchain ecosystem, BNB Chain, and offers services such as staking and an NFT marketplace.

  • Coinbase: This is a U.S.-based, publicly traded crypto exchange. It provides a platform for retail and institutional investors, including asset custody services.

  • Crypto.com: This Singapore-based company offers services including an app, exchange, DeFi wallet, and NFT marketplace.

  • Ethereum is a decentralized blockchain with smart contract functionality. Ether is the native cryptocurrency of the platform. Among cryptocurrencies, ether is second only to bitcoin in market capitalization. It is open-source software.
